# Project Spec — SaaSWriter Landing Page

## What We're Building
A single-page marketing site for **SaaSWriter** — an AI copywriter agent purpose-built for SaaS businesses. The landing page must communicate value immediately and drive signups/waitlist.

## The Product
SaaSWriter is an AI agent that writes copy for any content type a SaaS company needs to promote its product:
- Landing pages & hero copy
- Email sequences (onboarding, nurture, reactivation)
- Social media posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X)
- Case studies & customer stories
- Product announcements & changelogs
- Ads (Google, LinkedIn, Meta)
- Blog posts & SEO content

The key differentiator: trained specifically for SaaS — it understands SaaS business models, PLG/SLG motions, conversion psychology, and the language SaaS buyers speak.

## Target Audience
### Primary: SaaS Content Marketers
- Work at Series A–C SaaS companies
- Responsible for all content production
- Pain: too much to write, not enough time, generic AI output doesn't sound SaaS-native
- Goal: ship more content faster without sacrificing quality

### Secondary: SaaS Founders
- Early-stage, wearing all hats
- Pain: can't write well enough to compete, or don't have time
- Goal: get professional SaaS copy without hiring a copywriter

## Landing Page Goals
1. Communicate the value proposition instantly (hero)
2. Show what types of content it handles (use cases)
3. Build credibility (social proof / trust signals)
4. Drive action: email signup for early access / waitlist

## Page Sections (draft order)
1. **Hero** — headline, sub, CTA (email signup)
2. **Logos / trust bar** — "Used by teams at..." (placeholder)
3. **What it does** — content types grid
4. **How it works** — 3-step flow
5. **Features / differentiators** — SaaS-native vs generic AI
6. **Social proof** — testimonials (placeholder)
7. **Pricing teaser** — "Early access — free during beta"
8. **Final CTA** — repeat email signup

## Brand Direction
- **Aesthetic:** Liquid glass UI + futuristic dark theme
- **Mood:** Powerful, precise, modern — not playful or enterprise-stuffy
- **Typography:** Clean, technical, slightly editorial for headings
- **Color system:** Deep space dark + one electric accent (blue or violet or cyan TBD in prototyping)
